## Project Overview

**Garis Start Bayangan** is a dark romance/erotic drama novel in Indonesian, targeting 60,000-75,000 words across 25 chapters + epilog.

### Project Specs
- **Genre**: Dark Romance, Erotic Drama, Sports
- **POV**: Dual POV (Arya & Keysha, bergantian per chapter/scene)
- **Explicit Level**: 4/5 (Explicit)
- **Ending**: Dark/Tragic dengan Secret Promise
- **Setting**: Jakarta, SMA Merah Putih Academy

### Key Characters
- **Arya Pratama** (30): Pelatih renang, mantan atlet nasional, keluarga militer, menekan emosi, guilty over past
- **Keysha Alessandra** (18): Siswi pindahan, anak kaya, agresif mengejar Arya, kosong di dalam
- **Lana Wijaya** (24): Atlet junior dari masa lalu, sukses, merasa "berhak" atas Arya

## Writing Guidelines

### POV & Voice
- Dual POV (Arya & Keysha, bergantian)
- **Arya's POV**: Berat, guilt-ridden, internal monologue panjang. Prosa lebih lambat, reflektif.
- **Keysha's POV**: Lebih cepat, agresif, observant. Prosa lebih "muda" dan direct.
- Indonesian language, colloquial Jakarta style for dialogue

### Tone
- Act 1: Tense anticipation, forbidden attraction, slow burn
- Act 2: Escalating conflict, jealousy, frustration, spiral
- Act 3: Heavy, tragic, inevitable fall, bittersweet hope

### Dual POV Transitions
- Gunakan line break dengan simbol (`***` atau `—`) saat berganti POV
- Jangan ganti POV di tengah scene kecuali perlu

### Sensitive Content Handling
- Scene intim: Explicit tapi poetic — fokus pada sensasi dan emosi
- Scene "forced": Bukan tentang dominasi, tapi tentang dua orang yang hancur saling mencari pelukan
- Tunjukkan kerapuhan semua karakter — tidak ada monster murni
- Setiap aksi punya konsekuensi emosional

## Drafting Workflow

1. Check `notes/chapter-outline.md` for scene breakdown
2. Reference `characters/characters.md` for voice consistency
3. Reference `research/research.md` for factual details
4. Write draft in `drafts/chapter-XX.md`
5. Move to `chapters/` when final

- Push to origin regularly
